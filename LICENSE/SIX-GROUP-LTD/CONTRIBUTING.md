# Contributing to AIC Network

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

## Scope of Contributions

Contributions are limited to:
- bug fixes
- performance improvements
- documentation enhancements
- non-core feature improvements

## Restrictions

You may NOT contribute changes that:
- alter scoring logic without approval
- modify credit or economic systems
- introduce external monetary or financial mechanisms
- replicate proprietary AIC methodologies

## Review Process

All contributions are subject to review and approval by SIX GROUP LTD.

## Intellectual Property

By submitting a contribution, you agree that:
- your contribution may be incorporated into the AIC system
- you assign necessary rights for integration
- SIX GROUP LTD retains full ownership of the final system

## Code of Conduct

Maintain professional and system-aligned communication at all times.
